# BI-Dashboard-Cybersecurity-Training-Australian_Bank
Project Overview

This project presents a set of interactive Power BI dashboards developed for SecureBank to analyze and monitor phishing simulation campaign performance. The solution transforms raw cybersecurity training data into actionable insights that support risk reduction and behavioral improvement across the organization.

The dashboards provide a centralized, data-driven view of employee vulnerability, training engagement, and departmental exposure to phishing threats.

Business Objective

Monitor phishing simulation outcomes across the organization

Identify high-risk departments and employee segments

Track cybersecurity training completion rates

Measure click-through and failure rates during simulated attacks

Enable proactive decision-making for risk mitigation

Dashboard Features

The Power BI solution includes:

Phishing campaign performance overview

Employee click-through rate analysis

Training completion and compliance tracking

Department-level risk segmentation

Trend analysis across multiple simulation cycles

Drill-down functionality for detailed investigation

Key Insights Delivered

The dashboards enable stakeholders to:

Detect patterns of employee vulnerability

Prioritize targeted awareness training

Evaluate effectiveness of previous campaigns

Support compliance reporting and governance requirements

Strengthen organizational cybersecurity posture

Target Stakeholders

HR Teams

IT Security Departments

Risk & Compliance Officers

Campaign and Training Managers
